# Modern E-commerce Design System

## Design Philosophy
- **Visual Language**: Modern editorial aesthetic inspired by high-end fashion and lifestyle brands
- **Color Palette**: Sophisticated earth tones with surgical precision - warm beiges, deep browns, and elegant gold accents
- **Typography**: Bold serif display fonts paired with clean sans-serif body text
- **Layout**: Grid-aligned, scroll-native design with generous white space
- **Effects**: Subtle animations, hover transformations, and smooth transitions

## Color Scheme
- **Primary**: #8B4513 (Saddle Brown) - Deep, sophisticated brown
- **Secondary**: #D2B48C (Tan) - Warm beige
- **Accent**: #CD853F (Peru) - Elegant gold accent
- **Background**: #FDF5E6 (Old Lace) - Warm off-white
- **Text**: #2F2F2F (Dark Gray) - High contrast text
- **Success**: #228B22 (Forest Green) - Natural green
- **Warning**: #FF8C00 (Dark Orange) - Warm orange
- **Error**: #DC143C (Crimson) - Deep red

## Typography
- **Display Font**: "Playfair Display" - Elegant serif for headings
- **Body Font**: "Inter" - Clean, modern sans-serif
- **Accent Font**: "Dancing Script" - Script font for special elements

## Visual Effects
- **Hero Background**: Liquid-metal displacement shader effect
- **Text Effects**: Color cycling emphasis, gradient text animation
- **Image Effects**: Ken Burns pan/zoom, infinite image scroller
- **Hover Effects**: 3D tilt, shadow expansion, color morphing
- **Scroll Motion**: Subtle reveal animations, parallax elements

## Component Styling
- **Cards**: Soft shadows with hover lift effects
- **Buttons**: Gradient backgrounds with morphing hover states
- **Navigation**: Glass morphism with backdrop blur
- **Forms**: Floating labels with focus animations
- **Tables**: Alternating row colors with hover highlights

## Animation Library Integration
- **Anime.js**: For smooth transitions and micro-interactions
- **Splitting.js**: For advanced text effects and reveals
- **ECharts.js**: For data visualization with consistent color scheme
- **Splide**: For image carousels and product galleries
